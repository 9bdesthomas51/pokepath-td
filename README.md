# Pokepath TD

**Language:** English | [Español](README.es.md) | [Français](README.fr.md)

Pokepath TD is a Pokemon-inspired tower defense experience you can play instantly in your browser.

## Website
- Play now: https://pokepath-td.org/

## Highlights
- No account required, instant play
- Clean, fast gameplay experience
- Rich Pokepath data tools to compare towers and plan teams
- Tier list and Pokemon database for strategy planning

## What you can do
- Jump into the game immediately with no login
- Explore tower stats, abilities, and evolution paths in the database
- Compare tiers and plan your best team before a run
